üèπ Archer Piece

Location: Archers replace the pawns on B2, G2 (White) and B7, G7 (Black)

Movement:

Archers can move one space in any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal), but only to empty squares. They cannot capture pieces by moving into them.

Attack:

Archers can perform ranged attacks on Pawns, Bishops, Kings, Knights, and other Archers that are 1 or 2 cells away directly in front of them. The Archer stays in its original position after attacking (it doesn't move to the target square). Ranged attack targets are highlighted in red.

Defense:

Archers can be killed by any piece, including other Archers.

üéØ Special Rule - Knight Paralysis:

When an Archer attacks a Knight, a coin is flipped automatically:

  • Heads: The Knight becomes paralyzed and cannot move for the rest of the game
  • Tails: The Knight takes no damage and can still move normally

Paralyzed Knights appear grayed out and cannot be selected or moved.
